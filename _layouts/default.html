Shaswot Shresthamali

I am a doctoral student in my second year (D2) at Nakamura-Kondo Laboratory, in the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology, The University of Tokyo.

My supervisor is Prof. Hiroshi Nakmaura (中村　宏). I currently hold a JSPS Research Fellowship (DC1).

I was a lecturer in Sagarmatha Engineering College, Nepal from 2012 to 2014. I taught courses for undergraduate students of electronics and computer engineering. I also spearheaded the research division in the college and established an FPGA Research Laboratory under the Xilinx University Program in collaboration with Deepesh Man Shakya, Xilinx, Ireland.

I have an Master's Degree in Information Physics and Computing from the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology, The University of Tokyo (2016-2018).

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (with specialization in Digital Signal Processing) from Tribhuvan University, Nepal (2008-2012).

You can reach me at shaswot[at]hal.ipc.i.u-tokyo.ac.jp
